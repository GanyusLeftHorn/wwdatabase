Forte Circuit: Clarity of Mind Detonate
During Heavy Attack, when the indicator moves inside the [Frostbite] area, Sanhua charges toward the target to perform a Detonate, dealing Glacio damage equal to one Heavy Attack.

Ice Burst
Sanhua’s Heavy Attack detonates all [Ice Thorns], [Ice Prisms] and [Glaciers] along the way, dealing Glacio damage equal to one Resonance Skill.

How to Expand Frostbite Meter
The [Frostbite] meter expands with every 1 stack of [Clarity]. [Clarity] stacks up to 2 times at most.
Sanhua obtains 1 stack of [Clarity] upon performing Basic Attack V.
Sanhua obtains 1 stack of [Clarity] upon performing Intro Skill Frosty Thorms.
Sanhua obtains 1 stack of [Clarity] upon performingResonance Skill Eternal Frost.
Sanhua obtains 2 stacks of [Clarity] upon performing Resonance Liberation Glacial Gaze.
Expanded [Frostbite] returns to its initial state after 5s.
Upon performing Detonate, expanded [Frostbite] returns to its initial state.
