Small Device Missions Jinhsi's Token: III

A finely crafted small device, the surface is engraved with ancient text, the inner ring represents the Four Spirits of Heaven, which seems to imply directional information. "East, the Teal Loong, West, the White Tiger, South, the Vermillion Bird, North, the Black Tortoise". The outer ring represents the Twelve Earthly Branches, which seems to imply temporal information. "Zi, Chou, Yin, Mao, Chen, Si, Wu, Wei, Shen, You, Xu, Hai".
