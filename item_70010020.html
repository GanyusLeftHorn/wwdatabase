Map drawn by Hoochief. Missions A map that marks the locations of the cages in the eastern forest and the Hoochief camp.

The Hoochief of the Dim Forest, out of hatred for humans, will use tree cages to imprison passers-by. They use the hides of other creatures in the forest to crudely imitate humans and draw maps to remind the locations of the tree cages.
