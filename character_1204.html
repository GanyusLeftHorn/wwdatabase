When Mortefi's [Annoyance] reaches 100, the Draconic Hellfire can be cast.

Rules for Acquiring Annoyance
When the Basic Attack Impromptu Performance hits the target, [Annoyance] is restored.
When the Intro Skill Dissonance hits the target, 60 points of [Annoyance] is restored.
When the Resonance Skill Passionate Variation hits the target, 40 points of [Annoyance] is restored.
Within 5 seconds after casting Passionate Variation, when the Basic Attack Impromptu hits the target, 7 points of [Annoyance] is restored.
