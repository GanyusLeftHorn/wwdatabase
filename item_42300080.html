Coriolus Resources Material for cooking.

A special fungus that coexists with ancient trees, its medicinal properties and effects are recorded in various medical books in Jinzhou. A specimen of Coriolus is preserved in the Huaxu Academy, its history can be traced back to the time when the Giant Banyan in the Dim Forest was not yet grown.
