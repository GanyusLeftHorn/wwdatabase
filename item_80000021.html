Star Flakes Supplies Increases the DEF of all Resonators in the team by 28% for 30 minutes. In multiplayer games, it only affects your own characters.

A small food that the children of Jinzhou love very much. The meat is cut into a star shape and fried to golden, and it is eaten with a special powder. It is said that you can make a wish after eating it. "One star, two stars... you can't make a wish without eating it!"
