Resonance Skill: Magnetic Roar Magnetic Roar
The puppet "Zapstring" deals Electro damage to the target within the range, and puts Yinlin into the [Magnetic State], lasting for 8 seconds.

[Magnetic State]
During the [Magnetic State], the puppet "Zapstring" will be in the state of discharge, enhancing Yinlin's Basic Attack and Evasion Attack, and triggering [Judgment Attack] when dealing damage. Each Basic Attack can only trigger once, and can trigger up to 4 times.

Lightning Execution
Deals Electro damage once, ending the [Magnetic State]. When hitting a target with the [Sinner's Mark], it deals extra damage to the target.

Forte Circuit: Thousand Faces Thousand Faces
When Yinlin's "Judgment Points" is full, Heavy Attack is replaced by Thousand Faces, consuming all "Judgment Points" and causing wide-range Electro damage. When hitting a target carrying a "Sinner's Mark", the "Sinner's Mark" is replaced by a "Punishment Mark", lasting for 18 seconds.

"Punishment Mark"
When a target carrying a "Punishment Mark" is damaged, "Judgement Thunder" will fall, causing Electro damage to all targets carrying a "Punishment Mark", and can be triggered up to once per second.

Judgment Points
When the Basic Attack Zapstring Dance hits a target, it restores "Judgment Points".
When the Resonance Skill Magnetic Roar hits a target, it restores "Judgment Points".
When the Resonance Skill Lightning Execution hits a target, it restores "Judgment Points".
