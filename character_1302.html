Skills Basic Attack: Zapstring's Dance Basic Attack

Yinlin controls the puppet "Zapstring" to attack, up to 4 segments of attack, causing Electro damage.



Heavy Attack

Consumes Stamina, controls the puppet "Zapstring" to attack, causing Electro damage.



Mid-air Attack

Consumes Stamina, retrieves the puppet "Zapstring" and crashes to the ground, causing Electro damage.



Dodge Counter

After a successful Dodge within a certain time, using Basic Attack will attack the target, causing Electro damage.

When Basic Attack hits the target, it will leave a silk thread connecting the puppet "Zapstring" and the target, and grant a "Sin Binding" mark.

The "Sin Binding" mark disappears when Yinlin leaves the field.

Resonance Skill: Magnetic Roar Magnetic Roar

The puppet "Zapstring" deals Electro damage to the target within the range, and puts Yinlin into the [Magnetic State], lasting for 8 seconds.



[Magnetic State]

During the [Magnetic State], the puppet "Zapstring" will be in the state of discharge, enhancing Yinlin's Basic Attack and Evasion Attack, and triggering [Judgment Attack] when dealing damage. Each Basic Attack can only trigger once, and can trigger up to 4 times.



Lightning Execution

Deals Electro damage once, ending the [Magnetic State]. When hitting a target with the [Sinner's Mark], it deals extra damage to the target.
