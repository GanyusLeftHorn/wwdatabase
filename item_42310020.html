The crimson Dewvetch, scientifically known as "Jiang Dewvetch Hua", often collects natural dew in its buds, hence the name Dewvetch. Dewvetch is one of the main nectar plants of Huanglong, the nectar of Dewvetch has a natural, fresh aroma, and a bright, sweet taste.
