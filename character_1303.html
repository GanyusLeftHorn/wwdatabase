 Resonance Skill: Leihuang Master Thunder Wedge
Tap the Leihuang Master, Yuanwu summons the Thunder Wedge, causing Electro damage, and forms a Thunder Field centered on the Thunder Wedge.
The Thunder Wedge lasts for 12 seconds.

Thunder Field
When a character on the team hits a target while in the Thunder Field, the Thunder Wedge will coordinate an attack, causing Electro damage, which can be triggered once every 1.2 seconds.

Thunderblast
When the Forte Gauge is full, the Thunder Wedge will cast Thunderblast, causing Electro damage.
Rumbling Spark
When the Forte is full, hold the Resonance Skill Leihuang Master to consume all Forte, cast Rumbling Spark, causing Electro damage, and enter the Lightning Infused state, which lasts for 10 seconds.
If there is a Resonance Skill: Thunder Wedge on the field, casting Rumbling Spark will immediately detonate the Resonance Skill: Thunder Wedge, causing additional Electro damage (this damage is Resonance Skill damage).
(The rules for obtaining Forte and the detailed mechanism of the Lightning Infused state can be found in the Forte Circuit)

 Outro Skill: Drive Thunder Lightning strikes centered on the skill target, causing significant Vibration Strength damage to the hit enemies. Forte Circuit: Unassuming Thunder Rumbling Spark
When [Readiness] is full, long press the Resonance Skill Thunder Fist to consume all [Readiness] and cast Rumbling Spark, causing Electro damage and entering the Lightning Infused state, lasting for 10 seconds.
If there is a Resonance Skill: Thunder Wedge on the field, casting Rumbling Spark will immediately detonate the Resonance Skill: Thunder Wedge, causing additional Electro damage (this damage is Resonance Skill damage).

Lightning Infused
The character in the Lightning Infused state has a greatly increased resistance to interruption, and when Yuanwu is in this state:
: The attack range of Basic Attack is increased, causing Electro damage, and the ability to reduce the resonance of monsters is increased;
: The attack of Heavy Attack is more rapid, causing Electro damage, and the ability to reduce the resonance of monsters is increased;
: Dodge and counterattack, causing Electro damage, and the ability to reduce the resonance of monsters is increased.

Edge Acquisition Rules
When the Resonance Skill Thunder Wedge is on the field, [Readiness] will slowly recover, and this effect will still take effect when Yuanwu is in the background;
When the Resonance Skill Thunder Wedge is on the field, it will recover 6 points of [Readiness] for Yuanwu every second;
When the Resonance Skill Thunder Wedge hits the target in a coordinated attack, it will recover 5 points of [Readiness].
