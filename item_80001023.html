Lotus Seed Soup Supplies Increases the attack of all Resonators in the team by 120 points and Crit. Rate by 6% for 30 minutes, only effective for the player's Character in multiplayer games.

Maintaining a healthy body is crucial. The combination of gold and silver lotus seeds in this recipe provides warmth, nourishment, and a calming effect on both the body and mind. It was the first medicinal meal Jianxin learned during her mountain retreat and remains one of her go-to recipes for overall health and well-being.
