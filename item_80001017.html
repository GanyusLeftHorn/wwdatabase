Golden Fried Rice Supplies Increase the DEF of all Resonators in the team by 28%, lasting 30 minutes. In multiplayer games, it only affects the player's own Character.

Fried rice is a staple dish for beginner cooks, but only those with a discerning palate can master it to perfection. Yinlin, accustomed to the affluent and refined through her job, has developed a taste for the finest cuisine. This golden fried rice, made with humble ingredients but expert skill, is her favorite. It embodies what she values most in cooking - the ability to turn simplicity into excellence.
