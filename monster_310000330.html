Traffic Illuminator Element: Spectro

The faithful followers of Fractsidus. For those who lack the talent of resonance but thirst for power, Fractsidus has crossed the forbidden line of science, coupling human beings with the limbs of Tacet Discord through special mechanical parts, causing the followers' bodies to grow strong and powerful gun limbs. The frequency of Tacet Discord is increasingly distorting the followers' minds, eroding their bodies. They choose to wrap themselves in bandages to hide their ugly faces.
