Stuffed Tofu Supplies Increase the ATK of all Resonators in the team by 110 points and 25% Crit. Rate for 30 minutes. In multiplayer games, it only affects your own characters.

A private dish made by digging a hole in the tofu and filling it with special minced meat. The tofu that is dug out should not be wasted and can be made into other tofu snacks.
