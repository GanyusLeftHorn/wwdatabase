Kudzu Congee Supplies Increases the attack of all Resonators in the team by 150 points for 30 minutes, only effective for the player's Character in multiplayer games.

A porridge with kudzu powder, it has a strong sense of fullness. In the early years, there were always peddlers with shoulder poles in the streets and alleys of Jinzhou. When they knocked out the sound of "ding ding dang", they knew it was for selling Kudzu Congee. Therefore, the elderly also use "ding ding dang" to refer to Kudzu Congee.
